ABOUT DJ LC

aka..World Famous dj lc is one of Lagos most sought-after female DJS; and has quickly established herself as one of the hottest in the industry. Playing in venues all around the East Coast, bringing a sound of haunting vocals mixed with heart-pounding beats and rhythms, as well as producing fresh events spotlighting women in hip hop and guesting at many premier music nights and venues. Also found behind the turntables at weddings, private engagements, and corporate events with residencies at several local venues, she can also be seen anchoring statewide events from Lagos to all over the world. She has provided the soundtrack for special events, changed the landscape and continues to shine; in addition to working with brands, Lc is well known in her home base of lagos, but has graced stages around the country and abroad, hitting colleges and festivals from coast to coast and fortifying her followers from all around the globe.

Beyond Entertaining hundreds of djs, engagements annually, she has dedicated time to hosting dj workshops, at various local homeless shelters for women and youth,contributing dj lessons, her well known logo and merch line has also made a name for itself,allowing lc's fans to show support from anywhere and contribute to her philanthropic efforts by way of profits percentage donations.

she is in the high demand,mixing hip hop,r&b,rap,trap,reggae,deep house and top 40,shes one of the vibe supplier for any music addict.
